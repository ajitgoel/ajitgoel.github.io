Technical Challenge & Development Orchestration: CAAS

April 16, 2026 • 9 min read • System Design

Ensuring that the zones and their components on the personalized health homepage could be dynamically configured based on each user’s profile presented a significant architectural challenge. We needed a solution that offered high flexibility and maintainability, allowing for configuration changes without the overhead of application redeployments.

The Architectural Crossroads

We evaluated three primary paths for implementing this dynamic Content As A Service (CAAS) orchestration:

Option 1

Drools Rules Engine

While Drools is an incredibly powerful and flexible choice for complex business rule management, we found it introduced unnecessary complexity and significant performance overhead for our architectural requirements.

Option 2

Open Source Rule Engines

We looked at several other engines like Easy Rules. Our findings were consistent: they were either over-engineered for our needs or lacked the specific features required for seamless integration with our health data layer.

Option 3

Java-based SpEL Engine

Leveraging Spring Expression Language (SpEL) provided the perfect balance of flexibility and performance. By externalizing the configuration, we achieved independent updates with minimal overhead.

The Verdict: SpEL with Externalized Config

We chose the Java-based SpEL engine. The ability to cache the evaluation results improved response times significantly, making it the most performant solution for our 1.8M user base.

Testing and Validation

The success of the SpEL implementation was validated through rigorous testing against three key criteria:

  • Dynamic Updates: We verified how easily configurations could be updated without application downtime.
  • Performance Impact: Measured the latency of dynamic rule evaluation, ensuring it remained well within our <200ms target.
  • Authoring Complexity: Assessed the ease with which product owners could understand and modify externalized rules.

Outcome

Development is complete. This solution empowers us to deliver a highly personalized experience while maintaining a lean, high-performance backend. By favoring simplicity and performance through SpEL, we've created a maintainable foundation for the future of Safeway's digital health platform.
